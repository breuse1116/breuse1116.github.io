Cheesy Chicken and Rice Casserole

Prep: 20 Mins * Cook: 20 Mins * Total: 40 mins

Servings: 8

Ingredients:

For the Cheese Sauce:
  • 1/4 cup butter (1/2 stick)
  • 1/3 cup all purpose flour (use gluten-free, if needed)
  • 2 1/2cups of milk of choice
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard or dry mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 8 ounces shredded cheddar cheese (~21/2)

    • For the Casserole:

    • 3 cups cooked shredded or cubed chicken breast
    • 3 to 31/2cups cooked white or brown rice
    • 1 (12-16 ounce)bagfrozen California blended vegetables

    Instructions:

    1. Preheat oven to 375°.
    2. Mist a 9x13 in backing dish with cooking spray and set.
    3. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, meltthe butter. When the butter is melted, sprinkle the flour over the butter while whisking. Whisk until it's well combined, about 1 minute.
    4. Reduce heat to low and add milk, 1 cup at a time, whisking slowly and continuously, for 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from heat.
    5. Add 1 1/2 of cheeseto the sauce and stir until melted and smooth.
    6. Pour cheese sauce into the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Add the cooked chicken rice, chicken and frozen vegetables. Stir until well combined. Cover tightly with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes.
    7. Remove foil. Stir, then sprinkle remaining 1 cup of cheese over the top. Return the dish to the oven and bake for another 5 minutes.
    8. Remove from the oven and let stand for 5 minutes before serving.s
