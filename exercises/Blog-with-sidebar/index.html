The Life of a Beagle Owners

The Wonders of Beagles

by Bonita Reuse

- Feb 20, 2026

Their Constant Hunger:

Beagles are NEVER EVER full. They constantly want to eat. Say you are leaving the house and wont be back til late, ok. You, being a good beagle parent, you dicide to leave a giant bowl of food. Hoping they will just nibble at it while you'e going, but instead they eat the entire bowl as you are standing there. One can not make this story up.

Their Constant Need for Attention:

Their need for attention and/or petting is UNMATCHED to no other created speciem on our big green world. Don't you stop looking, playing or petting them OR you will be sorry. They will gently or aggressively paw you to get your attention.

They Bark A LOT!

They bark at everything. They will bark as they dream. They bark at sounds, shadows, and strangers. They make it a point to announce their presence. As ANNOYING as they are, you'll NEVER find a more loyal family member than a beagle!
