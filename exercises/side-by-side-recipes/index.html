Tiramisu French Toast Pancakes with Coffee Anglaise

This isn’t just French toast with coffee flavor added in. It’s French toast reimagined to have all the components of tiramisu in a not so traditional way. The coffee flavor gets added in through the coffee anglaise which is like a thinner pourable custard. The mascarpone is incorporated into a white chocolate whipped cream on top of the French toast. You don’t even need to drown this in maple syrup and butter because everything else is just too good.
